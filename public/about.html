About Me

I began my career in healthcare as a Physical Therapist Assistant, constantly striving to improve the patient experience by designing better methods with patient feedback. Through this experience, I learned the importance of understanding the unique needs and goals of each patient and collaborating with a team to create successful outcomes.

Now as a UX Designer, I approach my work with the same focus on user needs and goals, aiming to design intuitive interfaces and experiences. I believe in the power of collaboration and effective communication to drive successful projects.

Drawing on my background in healthcare and passion for UX design, I am excited to create impactful and meaningful user experiences that improve people's lives.
